Acknowledgements

Since its foundation in 1996, more than 200 people have contributed to the development of the Czech Vegetation Database by providing original vegetation-plot data, digitizing data from the literature, standardizing, cleaning and correcting the data, georeferencing vegetation plots, preparing and updating the taxonomic backbone, offering conceptual advice, and providing IT support.
